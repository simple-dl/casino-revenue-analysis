# casino-revenue-analysis
This project analyzes 14 years (2001–2014) of commercial casino revenues across 23 US states.
Using industry data from the UNLV Center for Gaming Research, the study visualizes:

Long-term revenue growth trend

Impact of the 2008 financial crisis

Post-crisis recovery dynamics

Year-over-year growth patterns

State-level revenue differences

Regional composition changes

The analysis highlights how the US casino industry behaves as a cyclical yet resilient economic sector, showing its sensitivity to macro-economic shocks and its ability to rebound.

The main figure provides a comprehensive, multi-panel view of:

Growth from $27.1B → $37.6B

Shaded regions show growth, 2008–09 crisis, and recovery
